Massage services

Massage is available as an add-on to your apartment rental or as a stand-alone booking. Whether you want to release tension, restore energy, or simply relax, our experienced therapists are always ready to help.

Relaxing Massage

This gentle massage, perfect for those who want to unwind and restore their energy, uses smooth strokes and calming techniques that relieve stress, improve circulation, and promote relaxation. After the session, you will feel refreshed and relaxed.

  • 60 minutes — 110 CAD
  • 90 minutes — 160 CAD

Deep Tissue Massage

For targeted relief of muscle tension and pain, we offer deep tissue massage, designed to address chronic stiffness and discomfort in the deeper layers of muscle. It is ideal for those experiencing pain or tightness in specific areas.

  • 60 minutes — 120 CAD
  • 90 minutes — 170 CAD

Reiki Energy Healing

Experience the gentle yet powerful effect of Reiki — a Japanese energy healing technique that promotes relaxation and balances the body’s energy. This hands-on healing method helps remove energy blockages, restore inner harmony, and reduce stress levels.

  • 15 minutes on the go — 25 CAD
  • 30 minutes as an add-on — 50 CAD
