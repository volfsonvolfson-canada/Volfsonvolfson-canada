Soak & Savor at Ainsworth Hot Springs
Ainsworth Hot Springs is just a short scenic drive from Back to Base. Relax in the soothing, mineral-rich waters of the hot springs pools, and explore the unique natural limestone cave, where warm geothermal waters cascade through the grotto for a truly one-of-a-kind relaxation experience. After your soak, add locally inspired dining experience at the onsite restaurant. With stunning views of Kootenay Lake and the surrounding mountains, the menu combines fresh regional ingredients with creative flair, making every meal a delight.

Maybe add a visit to the Spirit Water Spa where my skillful colleagues offer a full range of signature treatments.

Exclusive Offer: Book a minimum 5-night stay at our Erasmus Suite and receive one free visit per person to Ainsworth Hot Springs pools, courtesy of us!
